ABOUT ME

JENI HAS OVER 5 YEARS EXPERIENCE AND ENJOYS CREATING UNIQUE DESIGN SOLUTIONS FOR A RANGE OF CLIENTS. HER WORK HAS BEEN PUBLISHED BY DESKTOP MAGAZINE, THE AUSTRALIAN GRAPHIC DESIGN ASSOCIATION AND THE LONELY PLANET. SHE ENJOYS WORKING DIRECTLY WITH HER CLIENTS TO PRODUCE GREAT DESIGN SOLUTIONS THAT HELP HER CLIENT'S BUSINESSES GROW. JENI IS ALWAYS ON THE LOOKOUT FOR THE NEXT CREATIVE CHALLENGE.
