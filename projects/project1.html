FRASER ACADEMY, STUDENT & PARENT GUIDES

Brochures designed for a Secondary School in Vancouver, Canada. Fraser Academy is a school for children with learning disabilities and dyslexia. The design is largely typographic in nature, and makes special feature of important information in an appealing, easy to follow layout.

When possible, illustration and information graphics were employed to communicate large quantities of information in an engaging way. Thus minimizing the need for large blocks of text.
